What to see and do in Orkney

Orkney's vibrant capital remains a Viking town at heart, with a skyline dominated by the magnificent 12th century Norse cathedral of St Magnus.

Scapa

One of Orkney's most popular beaches, Scapa is just a mile or so from Kirkwall and is popular with walkers and visitors

St Magnus Cathedral

St Magnus Cathedral, built from red and yellow sandstone, is of international significance.

The Bishop's Palace

Step inside the spectacular ruins of two remarkable residences at the heart of Kirkwall.
